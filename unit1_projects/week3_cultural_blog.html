Blog Post #5

What's your take on the DBC experience?

It is a beneficial program that will help set you up with the skills needed for a career in the development field.

What is your impression of DBC?

It is a quality program that teaching its students everything you need to be an entry level ruby developer. DBC is well known and has had many success stories. I chose DBC because it was the first of its kind and also the highest rated program.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I believe I have the skills and qualities to become a successful developer. I think this program will benefit me greatly. I enjoy how DBC teaches you to work with others because that is exactly what people will have to do in real world experiences.

Have your expectations of DBC changed? If so, how?

Not at all, I think it will be a great program for me and I expect it to benefit me.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

I am excited, but a bit nervous only because when it comes to coding I have always worked on my own and I tend to be, or at least believe, I am self sufficient and I like to do things on my own.
