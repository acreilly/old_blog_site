Independence & Confidence

When I was younger,(Ha yes, I am young, but I do mean younger) I was very dependent on others for my happiness. I couldn't do much for myself, had a low self esteem, and wouldn't really leave the house unless I had someone with me. I was very co-dependent on my ex, feeling as if I was not whole without him. It took until I decided to move to Europe away from him to learn who I really was and transform into an independent woman. I now have confidence, motivation, and love for myself. I am a driven individual who has a lot of potential to succeed in life.
