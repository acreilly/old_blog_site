Week Seven Cultural Blog

How do you feel when you think about your values? Do you think it can help you mediate stereotype threat if you recognize you are in it?

When I think about my values I feel as if I have my heart in a good place and I believe and live by positive values. I think my values can help me mediate stereotype threat if I am in it. My values such as cooperation, honesty, integrity, confidence, courage, willingness to help others, and openness can benefit me greatly when dealing with stereotype threat. These qualities will help me be able to speak up and confront the issue in a healthy and calm manner either if it is happening to me or the people around me. I treat everyone how I would like to be treated and believe everyone is equal.

Can you think of any other exercises you can use to help you when you are feeling down (for whatever reason) at DBC?

When I am feeling down, I use mindfulness, deep breathing, I'll go for a walk, listen to music, watch a positive movie or TV show, help others, treat myself, and talk about how I am feeling.
